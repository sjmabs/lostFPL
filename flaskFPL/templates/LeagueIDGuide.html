To find your Leagues ID is pretty simple and can be done using any web browser.

Begin by logging into your FPL account here.

Once you are logged in, click on the "Leagues & Cups" tab which will display the leagues you are in.

Click on the league you wish to see the ID for and then look in the address bar in your browser.

Here you can see the address of the page you are on similar to this example:
"fantasy.premierleague.com/leagues/123456/standings/c"

Your league ID is the number you see. In the example above the League ID is 123456.

You can repeat this process to find any ID of the Leagues you are in.
