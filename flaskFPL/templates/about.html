Lost FPL was created as a quick way for me and my friends to see who had the lowest score of the week.

It is a way for us to keep Fantasy Premier League more interesting throughout the year as we battle to avoid paying fines for finishing with the bottom score each week.

This makes players more engaged with updating their teams and continuing to play even though they may not be on the overall top spot.

You can use this tool in a similar way and tailor it to suit your needs.

All you need is your League ID and you can begin tracking who Lost FPL each week.

A guide to find your League ID can be found here or by clicking on the quick link "How To Find Your League ID".

There are a couple of options available to you when submitting the form.

Scores or Scorers?

Scores means it will return the players that have the bottom scorers ignoring how many players are included in this result.

For example, You may have 2 players on 20 points, 2 players on 30 and 5 players on 40 points. If you select bottom 3 Scores, this will return a result telling you that 2 people were the bottom score, 2 people were 2nd lowest and 5 players were 3rd lowest.

Scorers will return the bottom amount of scorers in your selection.

Taking the same example as above, if you ask for the bottom 3 Scorers it will only return you a result saying that the 2 players on 20 points were bottom and the 2 players on 30 points were 2nd bottom. It will tell you that nobody came 3rd bottom that week as the number of players you asked for had already been satisfied.
